    In the Arabic work culture, there is a focus placed on trust, morals and ethics when communicating. Qataris rely on closing deals verbally, placing an importance on verbal contracts rather than written contracts. Understanding the local culture, therefore, is imperative to having a positive and productive business experience in Qatar.

    Our Business Etiquette session aims to overcome cultural barriers and support eﬀective communication between employees, ultimately creating worthwhile relationships. The session provides an in-depth understanding of attitudes, values and mores of the local culture that are of signiﬁcant importance to the success of an organization.

Guests will experience the true taste of the season’s community spirit and celebrate with an Iftar of traditional Qatari cuisine.
